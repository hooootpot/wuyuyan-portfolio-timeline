AR HORROR GAME · 2023.12—2024.01

DARKENING

一款把现实房间变成“暗世界”的 AR 恐怖游戏。玩家以怪物猎人的身份，通过手机寻找藏在空间中的怪物，并在三块电池耗尽前完成捕获与图鉴收集。

An AR horror game that turns a real room into a hidden dark world. As a monster hunter, the player searches through a phone, catches creatures and completes an album before three batteries run out.

04 / PROCESS

开发取舍

DESIGN PIVOTS

不是删掉想法，
而是找到可玩的替代。NOT REMOVING IDEAS—
FINDING PLAYABLE ALTERNATIVES.

01

漫画风 → 写实风

COMIC → REALISM

早期探索漫画式视觉，但受 AR 技术表现限制，团队转向更写实的材质与光照，让怪物更可信地进入真实场景。

The early comic-book direction shifted toward realistic materials and lighting so the creature could sit more convincingly in AR.

02

手势捕捉 → 移动端交互

HAND TRACKING → MOBILE INPUT

测试 ARCore、AR Foundation、Hand Tracking Subsystem 与 OpenXR 后，手势捕捉只能在 VR 设备稳定运行，因此保留手机端可执行的交互路径。

After testing several tracking stacks, hand input worked on VR hardware but not reliably on the target phone, so the mobile interaction path was retained.

03

巨型怪物 → 漂浮眼球

GIANT CREATURE → FLOATING EYE

地面平面追踪难以支撑巨型怪物与爪部行走，最终改为漂浮的眼球怪物，并用呼吸动画保持生命感。

Ground-plane tracking could not support a giant walking creature, so it became a floating eye with a breathing loop.

05 / REFLECTION

项目复盘

REFLECTION

野心需要技术验证，
完成度来自及时调整。AMBITION NEEDS VALIDATION.
POLISH NEEDS FLEXIBILITY.

作为 3D 美术与动画负责人，我第一次系统处理 AR 环境中的模型优化、尺度校准和动画同步。手势捕捉没有在目标设备落地，但这次失败让我更早地把技术验证放进设计流程，也学会在时间限制内保留体验核心、快速重组视觉方案。

As the 3D artist and animator, I learned to optimise models, calibrate scale and synchronise animation inside an AR environment. Hand tracking did not reach the target device, but that failure taught me to validate technology earlier and preserve the core experience through fast visual pivots.

最终成果是一款能够完整游玩的恐怖原型：AR 元素自然进入真实空间，怪物具备明确反馈，玩家可以完成寻找、捕获与收集的闭环。

The result is a complete playable horror prototype: AR elements sit naturally in physical space, the creature gives clear feedback, and the search–capture–collect loop works end to end.
